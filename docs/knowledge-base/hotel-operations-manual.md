## Emergency Procedures

### Fire
1. Activate nearest fire alarm
2. Call front desk (dial 0) or emergency (119)
3. Evacuate via nearest stairwell (not elevator)
4. Assembly point: Front parking lot
5. Account for all guests in your area

### Medical Emergency
1. Call front desk (dial 0) immediately
2. Describe situation and location
3. Do not move injured person unless danger present
4. AED locations: Lobby, 2nd floor, spa
5. First aid kit: Every floor station

### Earthquake
1. Drop, cover, hold on
2. Stay away from windows
3. Do not use elevators
4. After shaking stops, evacuate if instructed
5. Check for injuries, report to front desk

### Power Outage
1. Emergency lighting activates automatically
2. Stay calm, assist guests
3. Front desk coordinates communication
4. Generator powers essential systems within 30 seconds
