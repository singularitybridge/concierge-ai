## Onsen & Spa

### The Art of Onsen

Our natural hot spring water comes from a private source in the Niseko mountains, rich in minerals known for skin-softening and relaxation properties.

### Onsen Etiquette

1. **Shower first**: Thoroughly wash and rinse before entering
2. **No swimwear**: Enter nude (small towel may be brought poolside)
3. **Tie up hair**: Hair should not touch the water
4. **No photos**: Photography prohibited in all bathing areas
5. **Quiet enjoyment**: Speak softly, relax peacefully
6. **Tattoos**: Welcomed at The 1898 (private onsen available for privacy)

### Private Onsen (In-Suite)
- Available in Mountain View, Onsen, and Sky Suites

- Available 24 hours
