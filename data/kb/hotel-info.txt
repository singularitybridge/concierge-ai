THE 1898 NISEKO - HOTEL INFORMATION

OVERVIEW
The 1898 Niseko is a luxury boutique hotel located in Niseko, Hokkaido, Japan. Named after the year the original structure was built, we blend historic Japanese architecture with modern luxury amenities.

LOCATION
Address: 123 Hirafu Zaka, Kutchan, Abuta District, Hokkaido 044-0081, Japan
Nearest Ski Resort: Grand Hirafu (5-minute shuttle)
Nearest Airport: New Chitose Airport (CTS) - 2.5 hours by car

PROPERTY FEATURES
