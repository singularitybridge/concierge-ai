THE 1898 NISEKO - SKI & WINTER ACTIVITIES

NISEKO UNITED SKI RESORT
Niseko United is one of the world's premier ski destinations, famous for its legendary powder snow. The resort comprises four interconnected ski areas:

1. Grand Hirafu - Largest area, best night skiing
2. Niseko Village - Family-friendly, tree runs
3. Annupuri - Quieter, great for intermediates
4. Hanazono - Modern facilities, terrain park

SKI SEASON
Typically December through early May
Peak powder: January - February
